Ice Skating Overview

Ice skating originated out of a necessity to get around in Finland. It was used as a way to save energy in order to cross lakes. In recent times, it has become both a recreational activity and a basis for several sports, using a variation of original boot and bone/blade.

Types of Ice Skating

There are several disciplines that fall under the general title ice skating. Some include:

  • Figure Skating
  • Ice Hockey
  • Speed Skating

Figure skating is a word used for a group of disciplines that fall under the artistic side of skating(see Figure Skating page for more detail). All displines of ice skating listed invlove the use of a a boot and blade used to glide on the ice. However, most people don't partake in any of these, simply skating recreationally. Either way, skating is a way to have fun!
